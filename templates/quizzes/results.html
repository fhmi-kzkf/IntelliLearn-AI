AI Tutor Explanation

Cross-validation is a statistical method used to estimate the performance of machine learning models. It works by dividing the dataset into multiple folds, training the model on some folds, and testing on others. This process is repeated multiple times to get a more robust estimate of model performance and helps detect overfitting.
